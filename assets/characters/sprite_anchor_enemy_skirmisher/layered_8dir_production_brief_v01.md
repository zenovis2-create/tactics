# Enemy Skirmisher Layered 8Dir Production Brief V01

## Goal

Migrate Enemy Skirmisher from monolithic eight-direction prep into a layered
8-direction hostile agile lane.

This lane is not meant to prove shield-heavy or knight-mass logic.

It is meant to prove:

- hostile agile threat first
- pursuit hunter second
- enemy skirmisher separation from `Tia`

## Anchor Requirement

Do not trust layered outputs as production truth until this anchor is created
and frozen:

- `source/8dir/anchor/enemy_skirmisher_anchor_8dir_sheet_source_v01.png`

All future variants must derive from that anchor.

## Read Goal

Enemy Skirmisher must still read as:

- hostile agile threat first
- pursuit hunter second
- lighter and more disposable than a named ally lane

The main risks are:

- collapsing into a `Tia` recolor
- becoming a generic rogue with no ranged pressure read
- picking up defender mass through upper gear

## Layer Guidance

### `base_body`

Keep:

- head
- face
- hair silhouette
- hostile lean body mass
- compressed aggressive posture

Must not carry:

- bow or ranged weapon
- shield
- swappable upper gear mass

### `base_outfit`

Keep:

- hostile hunter cloth base
- lower-body mobility silhouette
- stable enemy-side outfit read

Must not become:

- ally ranger softness
- bulky chest shell
- ornate hero costume

### `weapon_overlay`

Keep:

- ranged weapon only

This layer is required.

### `upper_armor_overlay`

Only use for:

- swappable enemy upper gear
- restrained shoulder or chest reinforcement

Must not carry:

- the whole skirmisher identity
- defender-scale armor mass

### `shield_overlay`

Optional.

Likely unused for Enemy Skirmisher.

If used at all, it must stay secondary to the weapon and agile posture.

## Legacy Rule

Any previous monolithic eight-direction prep remains:

- reference-only
- not final layered production truth
