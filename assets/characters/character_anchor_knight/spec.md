# Character Anchor Knight Spec

## Role

This asset is the primary style anchor for all human-scale Farland Tactics assets.
It is not the final protagonist design. Its purpose is to lock the world's proportions,
material language, silhouette discipline, and rendering tone.

## Summary

- Archetype: worn veteran knight
- Gender presentation: male
- Hair: short dark hair
- Face: clean-shaven
- Expression: tired, controlled, disciplined
- Armor class: heavy armor
- Loadout: one-handed sword and large shield
- Accent color: deep navy

## Visual Goal

The character must feel like a battle-worn tactical RPG knight rendered as a handcrafted
diorama piece. It should carry emotional fatigue and military experience without relying on
fine facial detail.

## Proportion

- Medium stylized proportion between chibi and semi-real
- Slightly enlarged head
- Broad shoulders
- Compact torso
- Shortened legs
- Large enough hands, boots, shield, and weapon to read on a zoomed-out battle map

## Silhouette Priorities

Three reads must land immediately:

1. Knight class
2. Defensive frontline role
3. Veteran wear and fatigue

Primary silhouette hooks:

- Broad upper body
- Heavy lower armor
- Large shield
- Straight compact sword profile

## Material Zones

- Steel: main armor shell
- Leather: straps, belt, boots, glove support
- Cloth: dark navy tabard, underlayer, or shoulder accent

## Shape Rules

- Large armor plates only
- Thick shield edge
- Minimal heraldic symbol, one focal area
- No thin chain, no lace trim, no tiny dangling ornaments
- Keep helmet optional in concept stage unless needed for silhouette comparison

## Shield Rules

- Large and readable
- Broad upper half, narrower lower half
- Central focal detail only
- Must remain legible when reduced

## Sword Rules

- Straight one-handed sword
- Functional profile
- Slight stylization is acceptable
- No baroque guard complexity

## Output Requirements

Required views:

- Front
- Side
- 3/4

Render purpose:

- Modeling reference
- Rhino blockout guide
- Style review baseline

## Review Checklist

- Reads as knight at small size
- Shield is visually important
- Deep navy accent is clear but restrained
- Heavy armor is obvious without clutter
- Face reads as veteran, not glamorous
- Can be reproduced in Rhino without relying on microscopic details
