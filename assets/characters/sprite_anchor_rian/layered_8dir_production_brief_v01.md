## Layer Guidance

### `base_body`

Keep:

- head
- face
- hair
- body mass
- arm and stance structure

Must not carry:

- sword
- visible upper armor mass

### `base_outfit`

Keep:

- under-cloth base
- lower-body cloth and boot silhouette
- stable non-swappable command cloth if required

### `weapon_overlay`

Keep:

- sword only

### `upper_armor_overlay`

Keep:

- chest armor
- shoulder armor
- upper-body gear mass that should visibly change
