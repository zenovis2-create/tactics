# Chain Lift Winch 01 Krita Cleanup Guide V01

## Purpose

This guide converts the current chain-lift-winch image into a reusable runtime-facing chapter prop.

## Expected Source

When a chosen chain-lift-winch candidate is placed in:

- `source/`

use this guide to produce the clean and runtime outputs.

## Cleanup Goal

Produce a clean siege landmark asset that:

- has transparent background
- preserves drum / chain / frame separation
- stays route-control-first and clutter-second
- can support chapter map previews, landmark cards, and icon surfaces

## Step 1. Remove Background

- keep full transparency
- remove all paper, glow, and framing
- do not keep drop-shadow haze beyond a minimal contact-softening edge

## Step 2. Preserve Major Structure

Keep:

- readable drum or wheel body
- visible chain run
- clear support frame
- one restrained accent only

Do not add:

- pipe nests
- clutter piles
- decorative gear fields

## Step 3. Tighten Value Separation

- drum must stay distinct from the chain
- chain must read before the accent
- accent must stay tertiary

## Step 4. Prepare Reusable Output

The cleaned winch should support:

- chapter landmark sheet
- map integration surface
- future icon extraction

That means the silhouette must remain clean and centered.

## Step 5. Export Clean Output

Export:

- `chain_lift_winch_01_clean_v01.png`

Store it under:

- `/Volumes/AI/tactics/assets/props/chain_lift_winch_01/clean/`

## Step 6. Prepare Runtime Derivatives

## Acceptance Checklist

- background fully removed
- drum, chain, and frame remain distinct
- silhouette survives 128px reduction
- reads as winch, not gate-control recolor or machine clutter
- sits in the same world as CH06 siege engineering and fortified ground
