# Sacred Staff 01 Krita Cleanup Guide V01

## Purpose

This guide converts the current staff support image into a reusable runtime-facing equipment asset.

## Expected Source

When a chosen staff candidate is placed in:

- `source/`

use this guide to produce the clean and runtime outputs.

## Cleanup Goal

Produce a clean staff asset that:

- has transparent background
- preserves shaft / head / wrap / accent separation
- stays calm and support-led
- can support loadout, support-card, and icon surfaces

## Step 1. Remove Background

- keep full transparency
- remove all paper, glow, and framing
- do not keep drop-shadow haze beyond a minimal contact-softening edge

## Step 2. Preserve Major Structure

Keep:

- straight shaft silhouette
- readable sacred head motif
- visible grip wrap break
- one restrained accent inset

Do not add:

- floating rings
- secondary staff heads
- oversized crystal masses

## Step 3. Tighten Value Separation

- shaft must stay distinct from head
- head motif must read before the accent gem
- sacred accent must stay tertiary

## Step 4. Prepare Reusable Output

The cleaned staff should support:

- equipment sheet
- loadout support surface
- future icon extraction

That means the silhouette must remain clean and centered.

## Step 5. Export Clean Output

Export:

- `sacred_staff_01_clean_v01.png`

Store it under:

- `/Volumes/AI/tactics/assets/props/sacred_staff_01/clean/`

## Step 6. Prepare Runtime Derivatives

Current derivatives:

- `sacred_staff_01_integration_v01.png`

## Acceptance Checklist

- background fully removed
- shaft, head, wrap, and accent remain distinct
- silhouette survives 128px reduction
- reads as support equipment, not spear or relic
- sits in the same world as field sword, paladin shield, and Serin-class art
