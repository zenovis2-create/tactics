# Hostile Shield 01 Krita Cleanup Guide V01

## Purpose

This guide converts the current hostile shield image into a reusable runtime-facing enemy equipment asset.

## Expected Source

When a chosen hostile shield candidate is placed in:

- `source/`

use this guide to produce the clean and runtime outputs.

## Cleanup Goal

Produce a clean hostile shield asset that:

- has transparent background
- preserves face / rim / strap separation
- stays coercive and suppressive
- can support enemy dossier, loadout, and icon surfaces

## Step 1. Remove Background

- keep full transparency
- remove all paper, glow, and framing
- do not keep drop-shadow haze beyond a minimal contact-softening edge

## Step 2. Preserve Major Structure

Keep:

- readable shield block
- visible rim
- clear strap support
- one restrained hostile accent only

Do not add:

- ally-style heraldic elegance
- giant spikes
- glowing effects

## Step 3. Tighten Value Separation

- face must stay distinct from the rim
- rim must read before accent
- ember accent must stay tertiary

## Step 4. Prepare Reusable Output

The cleaned shield should support:

- hostile equipment sheet
- enemy support surface
- future icon extraction

## Step 5. Export Clean Output

- `hostile_shield_01_clean_v01.png`

## Step 6. Prepare Runtime Derivatives

- `hostile_shield_01_equipment_v01.png`
- `hostile_shield_01_icon_v01.png`
- `hostile_shield_01_integration_v01.png`
