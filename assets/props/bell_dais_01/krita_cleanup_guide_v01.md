# Bell Dais 01 Krita Cleanup Guide V01

## Purpose

This guide converts the current bell-dais image into a reusable runtime-facing chapter prop.

## Expected Source

When a chosen bell-dais candidate is placed in:

- `source/`

use this guide to produce the clean and runtime outputs.

## Cleanup Goal

Produce a clean endgame landmark asset that:

- has transparent background
- preserves dais / bell / ring separation
- stays terminal-pressure-first and clutter-second
- can support chapter map previews, landmark cards, and icon surfaces

## Step 1. Remove Background

- keep full transparency
- remove all paper, glow, and framing
- do not keep drop-shadow haze beyond a minimal contact-softening edge

## Step 2. Preserve Major Structure

Keep:

- readable dais mass
- visible bell focus
- clear outer ring or plate
- one restrained accent only

Do not add:

- chain forests
- cathedral clutter
- crystal spectacle

## Step 3. Tighten Value Separation

- bell must stay distinct from the dais
- outer ring must read before the accent
- accent must stay tertiary

## Step 4. Prepare Reusable Output

The cleaned bell dais should support:

- chapter landmark sheet
- map integration surface
- future icon extraction

## Step 5. Export Clean Output

Export:

- `bell_dais_01_clean_v01.png`

Store it under:

- `/Volumes/AI/tactics/assets/props/bell_dais_01/clean/`

## Step 6. Prepare Runtime Derivatives

- `bell_dais_01_landmark_v01.png`
- `bell_dais_01_icon_v01.png`
- `bell_dais_01_integration_v01.png`
