# Robe Support 01 Krita Cleanup Guide V01

## Purpose

This guide converts the current robe-support image into a reusable runtime-facing equipment asset.

## Expected Source

When a chosen robe-support candidate is placed in:

- `source/`

use this guide to produce the clean and runtime outputs.

## Cleanup Goal

Produce a clean robe asset that:

- has transparent background
- preserves collar / sleeve / belt / hem separation
- stays calm and support-led
- can support loadout, support-card, and icon surfaces

## Step 1. Remove Background

- keep full transparency
- remove all paper, glow, and framing
- do not keep drop-shadow haze beyond a minimal contact-softening edge

## Step 2. Preserve Major Structure

Keep:

- readable collar shape
- sleeve mass
- visible waist or belt break
- one restrained accent only

Do not add:

- capes
- giant spell rings
- luxury trim passes

## Step 3. Tighten Value Separation

- robe body must stay distinct from trim
- waist break must read before accent
- accent must stay tertiary

## Step 4. Prepare Reusable Output

The cleaned robe should support:

- equipment sheet
- loadout support surface
- future icon extraction

That means the silhouette must remain clean and centered.

## Acceptance Checklist

- background fully removed
- collar, sleeve, belt, and hem remain distinct
- silhouette survives 128px reduction
- reads as robe support gear, not cape or banner
- sits in the same world as staff, Serin, and allied equipment support art
