# Archive Lectern 01 Krita Cleanup Guide V01

## Purpose

This guide converts the current archive-lectern image into a reusable runtime-facing chapter prop.

## Expected Source

When a chosen archive-lectern candidate is placed in:

- `source/`

use this guide to produce the clean and runtime outputs.

## Cleanup Goal

Produce a clean root-archive landmark asset that:

- has transparent background
- preserves lectern / plane / hardware separation
- stays revision-control-first and altar-second
- can support chapter map previews, landmark cards, and icon surfaces

## Step 1. Remove Background

- keep full transparency
- remove all paper, glow, and framing
- do not keep drop-shadow haze beyond a minimal contact-softening edge

## Step 2. Preserve Major Structure

Keep:

- readable lectern body
- visible slanted plane
- clear keeper hardware
- one restrained accent only

Do not add:

- giant book piles
- shrine clutter
- crystal spectacle

## Step 3. Tighten Value Separation

- lectern body must stay distinct from the hardware
- reading plane must read before the accent
- accent must stay tertiary

## Step 4. Prepare Reusable Output

The cleaned lectern should support:

- chapter landmark sheet
- map integration surface
- future icon extraction

That means the silhouette must remain clean and centered.

## Acceptance Checklist

- background fully removed
- lectern, plane, and hardware remain distinct
- silhouette survives 128px reduction
- reads as archive lectern, not truth dais recolor or furniture
- sits in the same world as CH09B root-archive pressure and revision landmarks
