# City Seal Dais 01 Krita Cleanup Guide V01

## Purpose

This guide converts the current city-seal-dais image into a reusable runtime-facing chapter prop.

## Expected Source

When a chosen city-seal-dais candidate is placed in:

- `source/`

use this guide to produce the clean and runtime outputs.

## Cleanup Goal

Produce a clean ritual-city landmark asset that:

- has transparent background
- preserves dais / seal plane / accent separation
- stays civic-oath-first and altar-second
- can support chapter map previews, landmark cards, and icon surfaces

## Step 1. Remove Background

- keep full transparency
- remove all paper, glow, and framing
- do not keep drop-shadow haze beyond a minimal contact-softening edge

## Step 2. Preserve Major Structure

Keep:

- readable dais mass
- visible seal plane
- clear civic center
- one restrained accent only

Do not add:

- giant statuary
- banner clutter
- decorative shrine overflow

## Step 3. Tighten Value Separation

- dais must stay distinct from the seal plane
- seal plane must read before the accent
- accent must stay tertiary

## Step 4. Prepare Reusable Output

The cleaned city-seal dais should support:

- chapter landmark sheet
- map integration surface
- future icon extraction

That means the silhouette must remain clean and centered.

- `city_seal_dais_01_clean_v01.png`

## Acceptance Checklist

- background fully removed
- dais, seal plane, and accent remain distinct
- silhouette survives 128px reduction
- reads as city-seal dais, not altar or desk clutter
- sits in the same world as CH07 civic ritual and public-pressure landmarks
