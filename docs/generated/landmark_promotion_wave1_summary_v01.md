## Why These Five

These five were promoted first because they satisfy the strongest mix of:

1. chapter identity clarity
2. small-size readability
3. low drift risk
4. direct value as object-icon candidates

They also cover multiple landmark grammars rather than overinvesting in one chapter only:

- investigation
- siege pressure
- hidden ritual
- sacred machinery
- archive truth

## Working Conclusion

Wave 1 proves that chapter-local landmarks can now move beyond support boards and into production icon surfaces.

The next decision is not whether chapter-local landmark promotion works.
That question is already answered.

- which candidate should enter `Wave 2`
- and whether runtime wiring should expand beyond `altar / lever / gate_control` for selected landmarks
