## Comparison Goal

The faction split should read in one glance as:

- ally melee: practical field gear
- hostile melee: coercive authority issue
- ally ranged: agile hunter support
- hostile ranged: pursuit-hunter pressure gear
- ally shield: stabilizing protection
- hostile shield: suppressive control block
- ally armor: fortress-like protection
- hostile armor: compressed authority pressure

The split must not rely on color alone.

## Current Read Check

### Ally Sword

- First read: disciplined frontline sidearm
- Must stay distinct from: hostile blade through guard geometry and value openness
- Drift risk: becoming too dark and collapsing into the hostile lane

### Hostile Blade

- First read: compressed coercive melee sidearm
- Must stay distinct from: ally sword through harsher guard and darker grouping
- Drift risk: reading like a simple recolor of the ally sword

### Ally Bow

- First read: practical ranger bow with a cleaner, calmer arc
- Must stay distinct from: hostile bow through grip feel and value openness
- Drift risk: becoming too severe and losing ally identity

### Hostile Bow

- First read: pursuit-hunter bow with a harsher grip break and darker mass
- Must stay distinct from: ally bow through tone and tighter hostility
- Drift risk: reading like Tia's bow with red trim

### Ally Shield

- First read: stable defensive class marker
- Must stay distinct from: hostile shield through noble restraint and broader protection read
- Drift risk: becoming too severe and losing the ally stabilizing feel

### Hostile Shield

- First read: suppressive coercive block
- Must stay distinct from: ally shield through wedge shape and darker grouping
- Drift risk: reading like a recolored paladin shield

### Ally Armor

- First read: broad fortress-like heavy protection
- Must stay distinct from: hostile armor through openness and less compressed authority feel
- Drift risk: becoming too dark and procedural

### Hostile Armor

- First read: compressed authority pressure armor
- Must stay distinct from: ally armor through tighter mass and harsher grouping
- Drift risk: reading like darkened ally heavy armor without posture implications

## Current Conclusion
