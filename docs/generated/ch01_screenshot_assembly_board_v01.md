## Assembly Intent

The board should prove that CH01 can show three different prop languages without confusion:

- investigation unease
- civilian rescue burden
- sacred or route-control contrast

## Included Surfaces

- `forest_tile_01`
- `memory_well_01`
- `scavenged_cache_01`
- `evac_handcart_01`
- `altar_01`

## Reading Order

The board should read in this order:

1. the terrain is quiet and traversable
2. the well is the narrative investigation landmark
3. the cache is the quiet search reward point
4. the handcart is the escort burden / civilian route anchor
5. the altar remains a different category from all of the above

## Chapter Fit

This board is meant to cover the visual middle of CH01:

- `CH01_02` escort pressure
- `CH01_03` investigation ambush

It should not drift toward fortress logic or boss-fight telegraph logic.

## What To Check

- CH01-local props do not collapse into altar language
- civilian burden reads softer than sacred objective language
- memory-well read stays quieter than altar but more important than cache
- forest support art does not swallow object silhouettes
