## Assembly Intent

The board should prove that CH06 can show siege-local landmarks without collapsing them into the shared interaction families.

The board should establish three distinct readings:

- fortified siege ground
- true cover landmark
- route-control engineering landmark

## Reading Order

The board should read in this order:

1. the space is fortified and militarized, not generic fortress
2. the shield wreck is the true cover landmark
3. the winch is the navigation objective or route-control machine
4. altar remains a different category from both

## Chapter Fit

This board is meant to cover the visual center of CH06:

- `CH06_01` beyond the smoke
- `CH06_02` battery line

It should not drift toward CH02 rescue tone or CH04 sacred-machinery tone.

## What To Check

- floor surfaces feel harsher and more militarized than earlier fortress spaces
- shield wreck reads as deliberate cover rather than decorative debris
- chain-lift winch reads as route-control engineering rather than generic machine clutter
- altar still reads as a separate sacred-object category
