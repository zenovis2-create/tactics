## Assembly Intent

The board should prove that CH02 can show fortress-local military landmarks without collapsing them into the shared interaction families.

The board should establish three distinct readings:

- engineered fortress ground
- siege-pressure landmark
- recapture identity landmark

## Included Surfaces

- `fortress_tile_01`
- `fortress_tile_02`
- `battery_emplacement_01`
- `banner_mast_01`
- `gate_control_01`

## Reading Order

The board should read in this order:

1. the space is fortified and engineered
2. the battery is the major threat landmark
3. the banner mast is the military identity or recapture landmark
4. gate control remains a different category from both

## Chapter Fit

This board is meant to cover the visual center of CH02:

- `CH02_02` outer-wall breach pressure
- `CH02_05` recapture identity

It should not drift toward sacred-city or archive logic.

## What To Check

- fortress tiles stay subordinate to object reads
- battery reads as pressure before machine detail
- banner mast reads as identity before decoration
- gate control still reads as route/system device rather than chapter landmark
