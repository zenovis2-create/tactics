## Comparison Goal

The faction split should read in one glance as:

- ally melee: practical field gear
- hostile melee: coercive authority issue
- ally ranged: agile hunter support
- hostile ranged: pursuit-hunter pressure gear

The split must not rely on color alone.

## Current Read Check

### Ally Sword

- First read: disciplined frontline sidearm
- Must stay distinct from: hostile blade through guard geometry and value openness
- Drift risk: becoming too dark and collapsing into the hostile lane

### Hostile Blade

- First read: compressed coercive melee sidearm
- Must stay distinct from: ally sword through harsher guard and darker grouping
- Drift risk: reading like a simple recolor of the ally sword

### Ally Bow

- First read: practical ranger bow with a cleaner, calmer arc
- Must stay distinct from: hostile bow through grip feel and value openness
- Drift risk: becoming too severe and losing ally identity

### Hostile Bow

- First read: pursuit-hunter bow with a harsher grip break and darker mass
- Must stay distinct from: ally bow through tone and tighter hostility
- Drift risk: reading like Tia's bow with red trim
