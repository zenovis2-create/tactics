# Records Evidence Family Polish Review V01

## Purpose

This document defines the polish-review standard for the current records-facing
family pair:

- `evidence`
- `bell`

It evaluates the authored `Records > Evidence` entries as presentation surfaces,
not as gameplay systems.

## Scope

Current authored anchors:

- `evidence` -> `CH05_03`
- `bell` -> `CH07_01`

Current destination:

- `Records > Evidence`

## Review Targets

Review each entry across:

1. summary sentence clarity
2. family identity preservation
3. separation from nearby families
4. fit with records/codex tone rather than briefing tone

## Family-Specific Review Standard

### `evidence`

The records entry should read as:

- archive authority
- witness logic
- controlled truth access

It should not read as:

- generic sacred relic text
- battle objective instruction
- abstract lore unrelated to record control

Fast review questions:

- does the entry still feel like controlled truth, not just hidden truth
- does it stay distinct from `altar` and from general archive atmosphere

### `bell`

The records entry should read as:

- civic warning
- public order
- ritualized social control

It should not read as:

- generic machinery text
- immediate tactical instruction
- soft atmospheric city flavor without control meaning

Fast review questions:

- does the entry still feel like a public signal system
- does it stay distinct from `lever` and from generic city ambience

## Cross-Family Review Questions

- do `evidence` and `bell` still feel like different social systems
- does either entry drift into briefing tone
- does either entry overstate gameplay consequences instead of category meaning

## Quality Bar

The records presentation passes polish review if:

1. the family read is visible in the first sentence
2. the sentence explains category meaning, not player instruction
3. the entry remains distinct from its nearest neighboring family
4. the wording still feels like a records surface, not a battle surface

## Working Conclusion

The records-evidence polish pass should preserve category identity under short
summary text, not maximize drama or tactical detail.
