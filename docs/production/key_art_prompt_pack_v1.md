# Ashen Bell Key Art Prompt Pack v1

## Purpose

Provide ready-to-use prompt variants for spoiler-safe vertical slice key art generation and art exploration.

These prompts should stay within the same world and visual language as the current battle slice:
- melancholic fantasy tactics RPG
- painterly-symbolic battlefield language
- clear objective / threat readability
- no late-game spoilers

## Global Rules

- Do not reveal Zero identity
- Do not reveal final tower truth
- Do not reveal late-game memory twist details
- Do not use Karon/Karuon endgame specifics
- Do not make the image look like a generic mobile gacha ad
- Do not overuse bloom, lens flare, or glossy sci-fi light

## Master Prompt

Spoiler-safe melancholic fantasy tactics RPG key art, painterly-symbolic battlefield, two allied tactical pieces advancing through a constrained breach lane toward a golden objective marker while enemy pressure closes from the far side, readable tactical geometry, memory and forgetting theme, solemn military-fantasy mood, dark blue-black battlefield palette with gold highlights and pale cyan tactical glow, ritual arches and forest silhouettes in the distance, premium tactical RPG key art, no late-game spoilers, no anime pin-up framing, no glossy mobile ad look.

## Variant A: Tutorial Breach

Melancholic fantasy tactics RPG key art focused on an opening breach line, two allied units moving around a broken defensive lane toward a supply chest objective, enemy pressure forming across the upper board, tactical squares implied but not overdrawn, dark navy battlefield base, brass-gold objective glow, restrained painted atmosphere, premium and readable, spoiler-safe.

## Variant B: Forest Reconnaissance

Spoiler-safe tactics RPG key art, Greenwood reconnaissance mood, two allied tactical pieces threading through forest cover toward marked trail posts, enemy silhouettes holding the safer terrain farther up the lane, soft green-black forest palette with pale tactical cyan and muted gold markers, painterly but readable, premium tactics-game framing.

## Variant C: Ritual City Pressure

Spoiler-safe tactical RPG hero art, ritual-city battlefield under sermon pressure, two allied pieces at the lower approach while one marked target is threatened by a ritual ring, cathedral arcs and hymn geometry in the distance, dark violet-black palette with pale cyan tactical lines and gold objective language, solemn, premium, not flashy.

## Variant D: Contact Sheet Direction

Use four small frame studies on one board:
- Tutorial breach
- CH03 forest recon
- CH07 ritual threat
- CH10 end-state pressure

The board should demonstrate that the game has a consistent visual system across multiple spaces without exposing late-game spoilers.

## Negative Prompt / Avoid List

- no chibi proportions
- no giant character faces
- no floating JRPG portrait collage
- no over-detailed armor rendering
- no photorealism
- no bright candy palette
- no generic sci-fi HUD overlays
- no spoiler architecture from the final truth sequence

## Output Preference

If generating variations, prioritize:
1. one hero key art
2. one alternate composition
3. one mood board / contact-sheet direction
