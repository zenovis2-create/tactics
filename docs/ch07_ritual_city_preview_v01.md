# CH07 Ritual City Preview V01

## Purpose

This preview validates whether the promoted interaction-object family still separates correctly inside a ritual-city pressure surface.

It asks:

`Can altar, lever, and gate-control stay distinct when the map shifts into civic ritual pressure instead of fortress or archive logic?`

## Required Ingredients

The frame should include:

- `fortress_tile_01`
- `fortress_tile_02`
- `altar_01`
- `lever_01`
- `gate_control_01`
- `Rian`
- `Serin`
- `Tia`
- `Enemy Raider`
- `Enemy Skirmisher`

## Read Targets

- altar: prayer / memory / civic oath anchor
- lever: local release or hand-operated city mechanism
- gate-control: broader route-control infrastructure

## Pass Conditions

- the city surface does not collapse altar into generic machinery
- lever still reads as local and immediate
- gate-control still reads as larger route logic
- units remain the primary screen read
